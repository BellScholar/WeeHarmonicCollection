Korean Ballad

Because I am originally from South Korea, I do listen to a lot of Korean songs, especially Korean ballad songs. I was influenced by my parents who used to listen to these songs.

My friends told me why listen to sad songs when you can listen to exciting songs that can cheer you up? I don't just listen to Korean ballad songs because I want to listen to sad songs, I listen to these because I was amazed on how a type of music can touch one's heart.
