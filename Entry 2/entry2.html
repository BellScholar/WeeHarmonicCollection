MY HOME

Because I am originally from South Korea, I do listen to a lot of Korean songs, especially Korean ballad songs. I was influenced by my parents who used to listen to these songs.

MY TASTE IN MUSIC

My friends told me why listen to sad songs when you can listen to happy and fun songs that can cheer you up? I don't just listen to Korean ballad songs because I want to listen to sad songs. I listen to these because I was amazed on how a type of music can touch one's heart. I also feel comforted when I am listening to these songs.
